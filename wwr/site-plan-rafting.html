Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to encourage viewers to schedule a white water rafting trip with Great Adventure. It also provides some safety information about White Water Rafting.

Audience

The intended audience of this site are those looking to book a guided white water rafting trip with the company Great Adventure. It provides prices, scheduling, and safety information about rafting for potential clients.
